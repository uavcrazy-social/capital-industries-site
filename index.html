Season 3 features

Built for players who want more than a short two-week progression loop.

Factory and tech progression

Automation is the core identity, but progression is not limited to Create. Season 3 expands into broader tech systems, infrastructure, production chains, and long-term factory scaling.

Custom auction house

Player trade is supported by a custom auction house with listings, buy orders, and rank-based limits. The goal is a living market, not a disposable vendor loop.

Creative dimension

Players get access to a creative experiment space with plots for testing builds, machines, layouts, and designs without risking survival progress.

FTB claims

Protect builds, factories, infrastructure, and shared bases with claim support designed for multiplayer survival.

Daily quests

Daily objectives give players a reason to log in, progress, trade, and interact beyond raw automation.

Events built around modded gameplay

Season events are planned around PvP and Aeronautics-driven competition instead of generic minigames.

PvPClassic fights and seasonal combat events.
Drag racingAeronautics vehicle acceleration runs.
Formula racingTrack builds, handling, and driver skill.
Derbies & plane racesDestruction, flight, and chaos events.

Player community

Discord, shared projects, events, trade, claims, and creative plots are all built around keeping the server community active instead of isolated single-player bases on the same machine.
